👨‍🍳 Method
  1. Tzatziki: grate cucumber, squeeze out ALL water in a cloth. Mix with yogurt, garlic, dill, salt. Refrigerate.
  2. Marinate paneer and veg chunks in olive oil, lemon, paprika, oregano and salt for 20 min.
  3. Roast potato wedges at 200°C for 25 min until golden.
  4. Thread paneer and veg onto skewers. Grill on high heat 3-4 min per side until charred.
  5. Serve skewers with potato wedges and tzatziki. Take Magnesium after dinner.
💡 Squeeze every drop of water from cucumber for tzatziki — it turns watery within 30 min otherwise. Greek yogurt provides probiotics that support gut-liver axis health.
